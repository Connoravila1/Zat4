Zat4 — test build

1. Unzip this folder anywhere (keep the files together).
2. Double-click Zat4.exe.
3. Windows may show "Windows protected your PC" — click "More info",
   then "Run anyway". (The build is unsigned during testing.)

Sign in happens in your browser; you'll land back in the app.
The two DLL files are the graphics driver bridge — leave them next to
Zat4.exe. Without them the app still runs, just less smoothly.
