Zat4 — test build (Apple Silicon)

1. Unzip, then drag Zat4.app anywhere (Applications, Desktop, wherever).
2. First launch: RIGHT-CLICK the app and choose "Open", then "Open" again
   in the dialog. (The build isn't notarized during testing; a normal
   double-click would be blocked. After the first time, double-click works.)

Sign in happens in your browser; you'll land back in the app.
